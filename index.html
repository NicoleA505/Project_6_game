Welcome! to Battle of Knights!

Instructions:

Pick up any of the following weapons and try to win the battle against your opponent!
Note: Each player begins with a Pencil for a weapon.

Each weapon has a different amount of power that will inflict a certain damage to your opponent.

Rules: You can only move vertically or horizontally, but not diagonally.

There are boulders throughout the map. You cannot move on them and you cannot move passed them.

To attack your opponent, move into a square directly next to them and it will activate the fighting mode!
